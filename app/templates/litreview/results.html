What is Genie?

Genie is a curated gene–literature knowledge base used by this application to store and share article classifications across users and searches.

When you perform a LitReview on a gene, each article is assigned a relevance category (Useful → Not useful). These classifications are submitted to Genie and are then visible the next time anyone searches for the same gene.

The coloured left border on each article card reflects the classification already stored in Genie for this gene, giving you an instant overview before you start a new review.
